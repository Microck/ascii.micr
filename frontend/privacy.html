Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

1. Overview

ASCII.MICR ("we", "us", "our") operates as a client-side web application. We prioritize your privacy by design. This tool processes images locally on your device using WebGPU technology.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your images.

  • All image processing happens entirely within your browser memory.
  • No images are uploaded to any server.
  • No user accounts or personal data are required to use this service.

3. Analytics & Cookies

This website is hosted on Vercel and may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics to track performance metrics (e.g., page loads, load times). These analytics do not track individual users or use persistent cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure.
  • Google Fonts: For serving web fonts (Silkscreen, Pixelify Sans, etc.).

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
